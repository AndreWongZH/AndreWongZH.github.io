This module in an introduction to hardware for Computer Engineering students. Students get to build circuits on breadboard and debug using a multimeter. We are also exposed to using oscilloscope and function generators that can test and prototype with alternating currents.

There are 6 hours of lab each week and 1 hour of tutorial lesson. At the end of the course, we are also required to build a robot that can navigate a maze. Knowledge of what we were taught in the previous labs was also needed for the project, such as infared red sensors and ultrasonic sensors.
